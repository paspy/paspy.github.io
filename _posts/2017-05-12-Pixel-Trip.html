Pixel Trip

May. 2017

The fast paced adrenaline pumping color tile matching game!
All you have to do is match a single set of four colored tiles on a grid as quickly as you can!
Try Grid Attack where the complexity grows as each grid gets larger, try Survival where you travel to each grid with a quick zoom, and finally play Time Attack where you have to race against yourself!
Post your High Scores and challenge your friends to beat it!
