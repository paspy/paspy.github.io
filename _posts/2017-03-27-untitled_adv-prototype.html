--- title: Untitled Prototype layout: post ---
March. 2017

Untitled is an adventure genre prototype game where one or two players survive in a random procedural generated world.
It's for an internship prototype test and took about two weeks to finish.

Feature Breakdown

* Procedurally Generated Terrain
* Random hexagon map
* Texture blending
* Gameplays
* Casting fire bolt
* Grab and throw rocks
* Hunting friendly pigs
* Support split screen two players
* Day-night shifting
